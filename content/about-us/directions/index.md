The Cathedral is located on the corner of Henry and Remsen Streets in Brooklyn, NY.

{{< street-address >}}

## By car:
The Brooklyn-Queens Expressway offers you exits at Tillary Street (coming from the North), or Atlantic Avenue (if coming from the South).  Follow Tillary Street to Cadman Plaza West, turn left and proceed to Montague Street.  Turn right onto Montague, proceed two blocks, turn left onto Henry Street, and look for the Cathedral on the left-hand side of the street.  From Atlantic Avenue (heading East), turn left onto Clinton Street, proceed to Montague, turn left again and proceed to Henry Street.  Turn left onto Henry Street and look for the Cathedral on the left-hand side of the street.

## By subway:
The 2 and 3 trains stop at the Clark Street Station in Brooklyn from both directions.  Walk south three blocks on Henry Street to the entrance of the Cathedral on the left-hand side of Henry Street between Montague and Remsen Streets.  The N and R trains stop at the Court Street Station from both directions.  Walk two blocks West on Montague Street, turn left, onto Henry Street and proceed to the entrance of the Cathedral on the left-hand side of Henry Street toward the end of the block.
